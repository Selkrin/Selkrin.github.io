Understanding Foundation Basics

The foundation is literally the base upon which your entire home rests. It transfers the weight of the structure to the ground and protects against moisture, soil movement, and other environmental factors. A well-built foundation ensures the longevity and stability of your home for decades to come.

Whether you're planning a new build, considering a remodel, or just want to understand your home's foundation better, this comprehensive guide covers everything from basic concepts to advanced techniques.
